Yogyakarta, Indonesia

18–19 August 2017

 @ichsugm  #ICHS2017

Held as a part of Universitas Gadjah Mada Annual Scientific Conferences (UGM-ASC 2017) series, the 3rd International Conference on Science and Technology (ICST 2017) provides an ideal academic platform for researchers to present the latest research findings and describe emerging technologies, and directions in engineering and natural sciences issues.

This year, five symposia will be held, the 5th ANNUM Conference, the 2nd Computer symposium, the 2nd Geomaritime symposium, the 1st OMICS Symposium: from Genomics to Metabolomics, and the 1st Engineering Science symposium.

The accepted papers will be published in journals that are indexed by Scopus or DOAJ/EBSCO: the Indonesian Journal of Chemistry, Indonesian Journal of Geography, Indonesian Journal of Biotechnology, or IEEE Xplore digital library.
