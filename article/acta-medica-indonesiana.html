Acta Medica Indonesiana

Acta Medica Indonesiana aims to publish novel and important research in the clinical area of internal medicine, including in tropical and infectious disease. The journal has been published by the Indonesian Society of Internal Medicine since 1968 and is indexed in Scopus.
