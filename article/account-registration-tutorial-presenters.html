ePaper account registration tutorial

This tutorial will guide you through the registration of your ePaper Management account.

1. Go to the ePaper Management site.

Alternatively, you can simply click the Register button on the ICHS website, which will redirect you to the ePaper Management site.

2. Below the login form, click Sign up here.

3. Enter your details and click Submit to create your account.

Notes:
  • Add your country’s phone code (e.g. +62 for Indonesia)
  • Enter only one active email address
  • You can change these details later (except for your email)

4. Your account has been created. Your user ID and password will be sent to your email.

If you still require further assistance, please feel free to contact us.
